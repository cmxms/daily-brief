## 📰 Last 24h — what happened & why
The conflict that started Feb 28 — which choked ~20% of global oil shipments through Hormuz — got a peace framework over the weekend. Trump: "Let the oil flow." That one headline did everything: oil dumped >4.5% on the Sunday-night open, equities gapped up hard, the war premium bled out. The catch: Iran says it's *still reviewing the text*, shippers are cautious on Hormuz transit (mine-clearance pending), and an Israeli strike on Lebanon is a fresh wildcard. So the rip is real but it's pricing a deal that isn't ink-dry — a signing extends it, a stumble snaps oil and the war bid straight back.
