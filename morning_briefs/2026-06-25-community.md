## 🧭 Regime read (generalized)
Both **QQQ and SPY are in negative gamma** (QQQ spot 710.8 vs zero-gamma 715.9; SPY 734.3 vs 735.9). In negative gamma, dealers *amplify* moves instead of dampening them — so a big gap up like this tends to **trend and air-pocket both ways** rather than mean-revert quietly. Translation for traders: **fading this gap is dangerous** here, breakouts can extend, and PCE can light the fuse in either direction. Calm VIX + contango is the counterweight (no panic bid), but the gamma backdrop says respect the volatility, not the calm tape. Narrow breadth = fragile: it leans on one chip name.
